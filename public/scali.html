THE TALE OF SCALE

In a town where life often went awry, Lived Harp and Raj, with dreams to reach the sky. They saw a neighborhood, in need of some flair, A place out of balance, in need of repair. Harp, the dreamer, with visions so grand,

Raj, the builder, with tools in his hand. Together they worked, their hearts in full sail, Creating a building, a wonder named “Scale.” Scale rose from the earth, its presence so grand, A beacon of balance, throughout the land. With rooftop gardens and windows that gleamed, It fulfilled the neighborhood’s dreams. But it wasn’t just bricks, wood, and rail, It was the love and the laughter that set the sail. Neighbors came together, their spirits set free, In Scale’s embrace, they found unity. Harp and Raj’s creation, both clever and cool, Inspired the people to break every rule.

They learned to find balance in all they would do, Living their best versions, their dreams coming true. The lessons of Harp and of Raj, let them prevail, To live in balance, to set their own trail. With dreams in their hearts and stars in their sight, They lived life in balance, both day and night. So, close your eyes now, let your dreams take scale, Find your own balance, let your spirit prevail. In the story of Harp and of Raj, heed the call, To live your best version, and stand tall.
Goodnight, dear dreamer, under moonlight’s soft veil, May you find your own balance, and always set sail
